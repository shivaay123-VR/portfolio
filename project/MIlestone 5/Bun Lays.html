How to make Bun Lays?

Ingredients Required-

  • Bun
  • Orange Flavoured Lays Small Packet
  • Mayonnise
  • Cheese
  • Half Onion
  • Butter to toaster

Method of making Bun Lays

  1. Cut Bun into two halves
  2. Apply Mayonnies on each side
  3. Add short onions to one side of the bun
  4. Add crushed orange flavoured Lays to the the other side of the bun
  5. Add grated cheese to anyside of the bun
  6. Add Butter to the pan and then toast the bun for two mins
  7. Then add Mayonnise on to the side of the the plate after keep bun lays on it

Now your Bun Lays is Ready !
