How to make Fruit Salad?

Ingredients Required-

  • Cut Fruits Different fruits-
  • (1/3)-Papaya
  • 1-Sweet Lime
  • (1/2)-Apple
  • (1/3)-WaterMelon
  • Dryfruits
  • 4-Strawberries
  • Mango Ice Cream

Method of making fruit salad

  1. Cut all the fruits
  2. Set them all in a bowl
  3. Then add your desired Dry Fruits
  4. Finely Add 1 scoops of Ice Cream
  5. Add grated cheese to anyside of the bun

Now your Fruit Salad is Ready !
