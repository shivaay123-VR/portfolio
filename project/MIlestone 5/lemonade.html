How to make a Lemonade?

Ingredients Required-

  • Lemon-2 medium sized lemon
  • Sugar-4 table spoon
  • Water
  • Salt-One Pinch

Method of making Lemonade-

  1. Put water into a glass
  2. Squeeze the lemon into a glass
  3. Add Sugar and mix it until it dissovles
  4. Add a pinch of salt and stir it well until it dissovles completes

Now your lemonade is ready!
